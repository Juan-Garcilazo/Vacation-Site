Puerto Vallarta

My first beach, my favorite getaway

Why it matters to me

I was seven years old when I saw the ocean for the first time. My whole family spent a week in Puerto Vallarta, and the mix of warm sand, bright sunsets, and lively streets shaped my idea of a perfect vacation forever.

Simple reasons to love Puerto Vallarta

  • Friendly waterfront: The Malecón boardwalk is full of art, music, and local food.
  • Golden beaches: Soft sand and gentle waves make it easy to relax or learn to swim.
  • Sierra Madre views: A short bus ride takes you from the coast to lush green hills.
  • Fresh seafood: Shrimp tacos at sunset are a must-try.
  • Cultural mix: Modern resorts sit next to historic cobblestone streets and colorful markets.
